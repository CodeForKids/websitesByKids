EASY

this guide will take you along the path of making the base of a website.

the first step you want to write in your textwrangler (mac) or notepad++ (pc) is !DOCTYPE html.

when you've done that, you want to press enter or return then write html.

after that press enter again and PRESS TAB and write head.

when you're done that press enter and then tab and write title insert title here title.

then press enter then backspace and then write /head.

after that press enter and write body.

press enter, then write /body.

press enter again, press backspace, and write /html.

this is the end of easy (also known as writing the base), the next steps of making the site are in medium.
