MEDIUM

this guide will now teach you how to start constructing the internals of the page.

the first step you want to do is to go into your body area and type p insert message here .

you can do that as many times as you want to write a paragraph.

after that you can write h1 h2 or h3 insert text here and then /h1 /h2 or /h3 to select

the size of the text. example: in your body write h1 derp /h1 and your text will show up very large

showing the message derp.

the next thing you can do is write i b or u insert text here /i /b or /u and your text will show

as either italic, bold, or underlined.

this is the end of the medium section. I will start to teach you some css in hard.
